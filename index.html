Story

My passion for programmming started in high school. My parents would give me two dollars a day for lunch and over time I would save up to 40 dollars to buy my first programming book. The book I chose was in JavaScript because I read online that it was a good beginner language. I would read this book during my lunch period and thought it was really cool that you could write instructions into a computer and then have those instructions turn into something that you see on the screen. As I gained more experience in coding, I notice that I started to incorporate concepts I learned from my classes into coding, such as two dimensional motion in physics. This interest and passion eventually lead me to major in computer science and then to pursue a career as a web developer.
